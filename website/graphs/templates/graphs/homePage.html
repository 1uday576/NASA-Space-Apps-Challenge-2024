Exoplant Extract

The goal of this website is to build various graphs based on the data provided by NASA Exoplant Archive to see the potential relations between data points, see trends, or an overview on expoplants.

  • Stellar Hosts - graphs related to the stars that contain these exoplants.
  • Exoplanets - graphs related to exoplants themsevles and there relation to others.
  • Stellar Hosts - graphs related to the discovery of these expoplants.
